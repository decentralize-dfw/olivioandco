What we found

The site does not
reflect the brand.

Olivio & Co has seven international design awards. None of them are visible when a customer lands on the store. That is the clearest sign of a deeper problem: a website that does not communicate what the brand actually is.

Design — The biggest problem

"It looks like every other Shopify store."

The site runs on a standard off-the-shelf theme used by thousands of other brands. The homepage shows a rotating banner and a product grid — the same layout a visitor has already seen on dozens of other stores today. There is no brand story, no sense of Mediterranean identity, and nothing above the fold that explains why Olivio & Co is different. A brand that has won the Red Dot Award, two iF Design Awards, and an ADC Bronze Cube deserves to look like it.

  • No brand story or value proposition visible without scrolling
  • Product catalogue is a plain grid — no way to browse by feel or collection personality
  • Award credentials buried on the About page; most visitors never reach it
Visibility & Search

"Customers looking for you cannot find you."

The search bar shows an error on every single page load. The blog — which contains genuinely useful articles — is completely hidden from navigation. Several live product links contain spelling mistakes in the URL. And there is no analytics tool installed, so there is currently no way to know how many people visit, where they come from, or where they leave.

  • Search broken on every page load — visitors see an error before they type a single letter
  • Blog hidden from navigation — invisible to almost every visitor on the site
  • No analytics installed — nothing about the site's performance is currently measurable
Trust & Conversion

"Visitors leave without buying — and there is nothing to stop them."

A parent about to spend €40–€60 on sunglasses for their child wants reassurance. They look for reviews from other parents, a clear returns policy, and proof that the brand has been trusted by others. None of these are present. No reviews on any page, no visible returns or guarantee information on product pages, no way to capture the email of a visitor who is about to leave.

  • No customer reviews on any listing or on the homepage
  • Returns and guarantee policy not visible on product pages
  • No way to re-engage a visitor who leaves without purchasing

How we fix it

Here is what
we will build.

Each problem has a direct answer. This is not a general improvement — it is a response to exactly what we found.

Design

A site built from the ground up for this brand.

The theme is replaced entirely. Not updated — replaced. Every page is designed specifically for Olivio & Co, with the Mediterranean identity, the award story, and the age-based product range as the starting point. The product catalogue becomes an immersive, filterable experience rather than a static grid. Shopping by age, by collection, or by activity will feel natural and considered.

  • Homepage opens with brand story, award credentials, and age navigation — all above the fold
  • Product catalogue rebuilt as an immersive browser with real-time filters and personality
  • All seven awards displayed individually, with their context — not hidden, not as small logos
  • Returns and guarantee policy visible on every product page, next to the buy button
Visibility & Search

Fixed, structured, and findable.

Every technical issue is corrected. Analytics goes live from the first day. The blog is added to the main navigation. Your existing articles — already indexed and earning their place — are restructured and properly linked to relevant product pages. Each piece is then forked into a newsletter version: a direct-channel adaptation that builds an audience you own, completely independent of search.

  • Search fixed; all URL typos corrected and redirected so existing links do not break
  • Full analytics with conversion tracking — live from launch day
  • Blog added to main navigation, properly linked from relevant product pages
  • Existing articles optimised; each one forked into a newsletter version for direct reach
Trust & Conversion

The tools that turn visitors into buyers.

A review system is installed and configured to automatically request reviews after purchase. Those reviews appear where they matter — on product listings and on the homepage. An email capture is added with a first-order incentive for visitors who are about to leave.

  • Review ratings visible on every product listing and on the homepage
  • Exit-intent email capture with a first-order offer to recover abandoning visitors
  • Returns and guarantee policy displayed clearly on every product page
  • Country routing fixed — blocking modal replaced with automatic geolocation
