The Story

Every collection carries
a piece of the Mediterranean

Olivio & Co was born from a journey — not from a boardroom. In 2018, while travelling through the Mediterranean coast, its founders came across a small, unnamed path lined with ancient olive trees, running between the sea and the hills. That path, that light, that sense of unhurried life in the sun — that became Olivio & Co.

"Named after a small path filled with olive trees along the coast. Every collection carries that spirit: the light, the colour, the sense of adventure."

The question that drove the brand was simple: why does children's eyewear have to be purely functional? Other brands offer colours. Shapes. Utility. But eyewear for children can also tell a story — it can be wearable art, it can be an expression of the world they're growing into.

That belief lives in every collection we create. The Coral Reef collection — inspired by the colours of the deep sea. The Creative Edition D — bold colour blocks inspired by Matisse. The Citrus Garden, the Greenhouse, Deep Sea, Olympia. Each one named. Each one with a story behind it.

We are based in Paris, France. We sell across Europe. And we remain the only kids eyewear brand in the world with a Mediterranean identity. No competitor — not Babiators, not Roshambo — has built a brand around a place, a culture, and a way of seeing the world.

Questions About the Brand

What parents ask us

Who founded Olivio & Co and when?
Olivio & Co was founded in 2018 after a journey through the Mediterranean coast — named after a small path filled with olive trees found along the way. The brand is based in Paris, France, at 231 rue Saint-Honoré.
What awards has Olivio & Co won?
Seven international design awards since 2020: Red Dot Award 2020 (Packaging Design), Junior Design Awards 2021 Bronze (Best Travel Products), iF Design Award 2021, D&AD Awards 2021 Shortlist, ADC 101st Annual Awards 2022 Bronze Cube (Packaging), iF Design Award 2022, and Green Product Award 2022 (Sustainable Design).
Is Olivio & Co sustainable?
Yes. Our packaging is 100% biodegradable sugar cane pulp — recognised with the Red Dot Award 2020. Our cleaning cloth is made from 100% recycled plastic. We continuously research sustainable materials for our frames and continue to develop more responsible production methods.
What makes Olivio & Co different from Babiators or Roshambo?
Three things no competitor has: Mediterranean brand identity (an authentic cultural story, not just colours), ski goggles (neither Babiators nor Roshambo offers them), and 6 age categories — the market's most precise sizing system. We also have 7 international design awards, named artistic collections, and a dedicated European store (.eu) for families across the continent.
Where does Olivio & Co ship, and how quickly?
We ship free across Europe on all orders over €69, dispatched within 1–2 business days from Paris, France. We deliver to France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Belgium, Portugal, Austria, Switzerland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and more.
