What we found

The site does not
reflect the brand.

Olivio & Co didn't win a Red Dot Award by accident. The product is exceptional — award-winning design, eco-conscious materials, a full range from toddler to adult. The question is whether the store reflects that.

Right now, it doesn't. Here is exactly what we found.

Design — The biggest problem

"It looks like every other Shopify store."

The site runs on a standard off-the-shelf theme used by thousands of other brands. The homepage shows a rotating banner and a product grid — the same layout a visitor has seen on dozens of other stores today. There is no brand story, no sense of Mediterranean identity, nothing above the fold that explains why Olivio & Co is different.

A generic store sends a generic signal. Customers who don't know the brand won't trust it.

  • No brand story or value proposition visible without scrolling
  • Product catalogue is a plain grid — no personality, no immersive browsing
  • Nine design awards buried on the About page; most visitors never reach it
Visibility & Search

"You're spending on a store you cannot measure."

The search bar shows an error on every page load. The blog — which contains genuinely strong content — is completely hidden from navigation. No analytics tool is installed. There is currently no way to know how many people visit, where they come from, or where they leave.

No analytics means no decisions. Every day the store runs without tracking is data you cannot recover.

  • Search broken on every page load — visitors see an error before they type a single letter
  • Blog hidden from navigation — invisible to almost every visitor on the site
  • No analytics installed — nothing about the site's performance is currently measurable
Trust & Conversion

"At this price point, reviews are not optional. They are the sale."

A parent about to spend €40–€60 on sunglasses for their child wants reassurance. They look for reviews, a clear returns policy, and proof the brand has been trusted by others. Reviews exist — but with fewer than ten per product, no photo reviews, and none visible on the homepage, they are not working. The guarantee policy is buried. There is no mechanism to re-engage a visitor who leaves without purchasing.

Every visitor who leaves without buying is a decision you could have influenced.

  • Reviews exist but are too few and invisible on the homepage — not working as a trust signal
  • Returns and guarantee policy not visible on product pages
  • No mechanism to re-engage a visitor who leaves without purchasing

How we fix it

Here is what
we will build.

Each problem has a direct answer. Not a general improvement — a response to exactly what we found.

Design

A site built from the ground up for this brand.

The theme is replaced entirely. Not updated — replaced. Every page is designed specifically for Olivio & Co, with the Mediterranean identity, the award story, and the age-based product range as the foundation. The product catalogue becomes an immersive, filterable experience. The brand's identity is consistent across every touchpoint — B2C store, product pages, and blog.

  • Homepage opens with brand story, award credentials, and age navigation — all above the fold
  • Product catalogue rebuilt as an immersive browser with real-time filters and personality
  • All nine awards displayed individually, with context — not hidden, not as small logos
  • Returns and guarantee visible on every product page, next to the buy button
Visibility & Search

Fixed, structured, and findable.

Every technical issue is corrected. Analytics goes live from day one — from that point, every visit, click, and purchase is tracked. The blog is added to navigation and the content that already exists is put to work. Each article is then forked into a newsletter version, building a direct channel you own completely independent of search.

  • Search fixed; URL typos corrected and redirected so no existing links break
  • Full analytics with conversion tracking — live from launch day
  • Blog added to main navigation, properly linked from relevant product pages
  • Existing articles optimised; each one forked into a newsletter version for direct reach
Trust & Conversion

The tools that turn visitors into buyers.

A review system is installed and configured to request reviews automatically after purchase. Those reviews appear where they matter — on product listings and the homepage. An email capture is added with a first-order incentive for visitors about to leave. The blocking country-selector modal is replaced with automatic geolocation.

  • Review ratings visible on every product listing and on the homepage
  • Exit-intent email capture with a first-order offer to recover abandoning visitors
  • Returns and guarantee displayed clearly on every product page
  • Country routing fixed — blocking modal replaced with automatic geolocation
