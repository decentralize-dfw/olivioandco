01 — Site Audit
What the current site
actually looks like.
We reviewed the live site at olivioandco.com directly. Every issue below is observable in the published site right now — nothing here is assumed.
Finding What we reviewed
Generic Shopify theme The site runs on a default Shopify template with a standard product grid. There is no custom interface — no age-based navigation, no immersive browsing, no brand-specific experience. This will be replaced entirely.
Search is broken "Something went wrong. Please try again!" appears on every page load. Visitors cannot search the catalogue.
Typo in a live URL Collection URL reads /collections/citrus-graden — a misspelling visible in the navigation and indexed by search engines.
Button copy error Sport Sunglasses section shows "VieDiscover" — a visible typo in the published page that sends clicks to Amazon, not the store.
Duplicate award image The awards carousel repeats the same image file twice. One slot is empty.
Social media links missing No Instagram, Facebook, TikTok or any other social link anywhere on the site — not in the header, footer, or homepage. Social channels are active but the site does not connect to them.
Blog hidden from navigation A blog exists at /blogs/news and is linked in the footer. It does not appear in the main navigation. Most visitors never find it.
Reviews not visible in listings Some products have star ratings and written reviews, but they are buried at the bottom of individual product pages. No star count appears in any product listing. The homepage shows zero social proof.
Email capture not optimised A newsletter form exists in the footer. There is no popup, no exit-intent prompt, and no mechanism to capture a visitor who is about to leave without buying. This leaves most visitors unretained.
Return & purchase terms Purchase terms and conditions are not clearly surfaced on product pages. Parents buying for a child need to understand the terms before committing — making this visible reduces hesitation and increases confidence.
Analytics & tracking Visitor analytics and conversion tracking have been reviewed. Whether current tracking is in place or missing, we will verify, install, and improve everything needed to measure what visitors do, which products they view, and where they leave.
Partner programme hidden A "Become a Partner" page exists on the site. It is not featured in the navigation or anywhere in the main experience — a channel for B2B growth that is currently invisible.

Phase One · Weeks 1–4
Design
The entire site rebuilt from scratch — custom code, not a theme.

The current site is a default Shopify theme. What replaces it is a custom HTML/CSS/JS build designed specifically for Olivio & Co — every page, every interaction, every layout decision made for this brand and no other. The quality of the interface will match the quality of the product.

Homepage
Built around a single clear opening: who the brand is, what it makes, and why a parent should trust it — before any scrolling. Age-based visual navigation (Toddler → Kids → Junior → Junior+ → Teen & Adult) so any visitor finds their section in one click. All seven design awards named individually — not just logos, but what each one recognises. Customer reviews moved to the homepage, where they actually convert. Purchase terms made visible. Social media links in header and footer. "Become a Partner" entry point added to navigation. The homepage is designed to be immersive and interactive — fluid 3D-influenced visual flow, animated transitions, and layered depth that draw the visitor through the brand story before they even reach the catalogue.
€ 2,500
Immersive Product Catalogue
A fully custom product browser replaces the default Shopify collection grid. 155+ products, filterable in real time by age group, product type, collection, and lens. Each product opens in a full-detail panel: multiple images, colour selector, lens options, sizing information, and a direct add-to-cart — no page reload, no redirect. Existing product data pulled directly from the Shopify database. Add to Cart and Buy Now as two distinct actions. The size guide opens inline on every product — parents get the measurement table without leaving the page.
€ 4,500
About Page
A brand story page that earns trust. Founding context, design philosophy, and why this is not just another eyewear brand. All seven design awards listed with name, year, and what they recognise. Sustainability credentials — sugarcane packaging, recycled cleaning cloth, FSC-certified materials — made explicit. A feature comparison against Babiators and Roshambo: ski goggles, European presence, named collections, age granularity — advantages that are currently invisible on the site.
€ 1,200
Size & Fit Guide
A dedicated page with a head-circumference table for all six age categories, measurements in centimetres, and direct links to shop each size. Parents buying for a child who is not present need to confirm fit before committing to a purchase. This page removes the most common reason for cart abandonment in children's product categories.
€ 800

Phase Two · Weeks 4–8
Visibility
Everything the site needs to be found by people who do not know the brand yet.

A well-built site that no one can find is still a problem. This phase builds the technical foundation for organic search visibility — and creates the content that captures parents actively looking for what Olivio & Co sells.

SEO Technical Setup
Every page title and description rewritten to match what parents actually search for online. Behind-the-scenes markup added across the full site so search engines can display star ratings, product details, and pricing directly in results — not just a plain link. The site's two domains (.com and .eu) are connected correctly so they reinforce rather than compete with each other. A site map and discovery file submitted so the full catalogue gets indexed properly. Visitor tracking and analytics reviewed, verified, and where needed improved or installed from scratch — measuring add-to-carts, product views, and completed purchases from day one. A cookie consent notice added that meets EU legal requirements. All URL spelling errors corrected.
€ 4,000
Five Expert Articles
The blog is currently empty. These five articles target search topics that neither Babiators nor Roshambo has written about — which means early, uncontested rankings. Each piece is 1,500–2,000 words, properly sourced, and written to be genuinely useful to the parent reading it, not just to the algorithm. Topics: why children need UV protection, the complete size guide from baby to teen, ski goggles versus sunglasses for children, blue light glasses for kids — what the evidence actually says, and protecting children's eyes on a family holiday. Each article links naturally to the relevant product range.
€ 1,900
Digital Growth & Visibility
Online presence reviewed and improved across key digital touchpoints. A 12-week content calendar delivered along with ten ready-to-publish post templates covering summer, back-to-school, and ski season. The brand's most common customer questions answered in a format suitable for publishing wherever the client chooses.
€ 600

Phase Three · Weeks 8–12
Launch
The tools that turn visitors into buyers. Then a full quality check before go-live.
Review System
Reviews already exist on the site — they are just invisible. A review platform is integrated and configured to display ratings prominently in product listings, on product pages, and in a homepage section. Post-purchase review requests automated. Once the review schema is live and ratings accumulate, Google search results begin showing star ratings next to the listing. Listings with stars consistently receive around 30% more clicks than those without — at no additional cost.
€ 600
Shopify Liquid Integration
The entire custom design is integrated into Shopify's theme system using Liquid — Shopify's native templating language. Every page, component, and interaction is wired directly into the live store: product data, cart, checkout, collections, and customer accounts all connected and working. Tested end-to-end before go-live.
€ 400
Conversion Tools
An exit-intent popup offering a first-order discount — converts roughly 8–12% of visitors who are about to leave into newsletter subscribers and future buyers. A save-for-later option on product cards — parents often browse for a child who is not present; this gives them a way to return without losing their selection. Out-of-stock products show a "Notify me when available" prompt with email capture.
€ 700
Testing, Performance & Launch
Every page tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop. All links, all filters, all cart interactions. Page load speed measured against Core Web Vitals thresholds and optimised where needed. Final client walkthrough before go-live. Full deployment to Shopify production. Client handover package: all access credentials, a brief usage guide, and a recommended roadmap for the quarter following launch.
€ 800
